About GPS Day

God's Prayer Squad (GPS) Day is an annual event where our community comes together to strengthen our faith through eating, drinking, and dancing together. It's a day dedicated to spiritual reflection, sharing testimonies, and lifting our voices in unity. We believe in the power of prayer to transform lives and communities, and this day serves as a powerful reminder of that.

On this special day, new coordinators for GPS will also be announced.

Joy Before the Lord

People from various areas, including nearby universities like MUCHAS, CUOM, and TIA, join us to celebrate this day through eating and drinking together in fellowship.

Testimony Sharing

In our GPS community, many members have experienced God's transforming power in their lives. During this day, some of them share their powerful testimonies.

Coordinator Announcement

During this special and dedicated day, the new GPS coordinators are announced, marking the beginning of a new season of leadership.

Why It's Important to Join Us

"Let us not give up meeting together... but let us encourage one another." (Hebrews 10:25)

Every moment spent together reminds us of life's fragility and sacredness.
Coming together invites perspective, awe, and renewed commitment to live with purpose and compassion.
