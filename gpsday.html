About Gps Day

God's Prayer Squad (GPS) Day is an annual event where our community comes together to strengthen our faith through collective Eating, Drinking, and Dancing together. It's a day dedicated to spiritual reflection, sharing testimonies, and lifting our voices in unity. We believe in the power of prayer to transform lives and communities, and this day serves as a powerful reminder of that.
