Our History

God's Prayer Squad (GPS) is a dynamic family of God's children united by a divine calling to stand in the gap through fervent, united prayer. Born out of a deep passion for intercession, GPS began as a small group of believers committed to praying for the church, our communities, our nation Tanzania, and the world at large.

Rooted in the belief that prayer changes things, the community grew from humble beginnings into a vibrant movement. Members gather regularly for intense prayer sessions, spiritual warfare, worship, and encouragement, always seeking God's face for guidance, revival, and breakthroughs. GPS emphasizes personal spiritual growth, unity among believers, and service to society through prayer-backed action.

From its early days in Tanzania (with strong presence in areas like Mbeya and Dar es Salaam), GPS has impacted countless lives through seasons of focused themes, powerful teachings, and communal support.

Today, GPS continues to evolve while staying true to its core: To pray for the will of God to be done on earth as it is in heaven. We invite all who feel the call to join this squad and become part of a movement that believes prayer is the key to transformation.
