Our Charity Mission
Faith in Action

On this special day, we came together as God's Prayer Squad to make a tangible difference in our community. We believe that prayer, when combined with action, can create miracles. Our annual Charity Day is a testament to this belief, bringing us closer to those in need and allowing us to be the hands and feet of our faith.

Our Charity Pillars

Food for the Hungry

GPS also contributes food necessities to various centers for people in need.

Education for All

GPS provides various essential school supplies for students, including exercise books, school uniforms, and many other necessary student items.

Healthcare Outreach

GPS contributes various items that promote good health, such as toothbrushes, toothpaste, and other necessities.

Shelter and Hope

GPS makes donations of items that help people during cold weather, including blankets and sweaters.
GPS interacts with the needy, sharing meals and playing together to foster happiness and strong hope among them
