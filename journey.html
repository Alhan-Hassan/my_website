Our Journey: From Humble Beginnings to Global Impact

The GPS Prayer Community—standing for God's Prayer Squad—began as a small group of believers in Mbeya University of Science and Tehnology (MUST), Tanzania, united by a shared vision: to reach people worldwide and reveal the transformative power of God through prayer. Inspired by Jesus' words in Matthew 18:20, "For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them," we started in 2020 as a local prayer circle during challenging times.

"Ask and it will be given to you; seek and you will find; knock and the door will be opened to you." — Matthew 7:7

Our main mission is clear: to connect hearts across continents, showing that prayer isn't just words—it's a direct line to God's mighty power, capable of healing, guiding, and changing lives eternally.

Our Mission: Reaching the World with God's Power

At the core of GPS is a passion to make the power of God known globally through prayer. We believe prayer bridges gaps—cultural, geographical, and spiritual—inviting everyone to encounter Jesus' love and authority.

  • Global Reach: Using technology to connect pray-ers from every continent, fostering unity in diversity.
  • Empowering Testimonies: Sharing stories of God's miracles to inspire faith and draw seekers closer to Him.
  • Practical Prayer: Teaching biblical prayer strategies that lead to real change, from personal revival to societal transformation.
  • Inclusive Community: Welcoming all, regardless of background, to experience the Holy Spirit's power in prayer.

Through GPS, we've seen God heal the sick, restore families, and bring hope to the hopeless. Our goal? To ignite a worldwide prayer revolution where every person knows: God hears, God moves, God transforms.

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations..." — Matthew 28:19
